How distances work
Distance is estimated from bounding-box width using a focal-length heuristic calibrated for a typical laptop webcam. Accuracy improves for larger objects (person, car). For precise measurements a depth camera is recommended.

How the beep works
Proximity Beep watches the bounding-box area. Bigger boxes mean closer objects, so the beep interval shortens as urgency increases.

How spatial audio works
When enabled, the beep is panned based on the object center X position: left third, center third, or right third of the screen.

How environment mode works
It filters spoken and audio alerts to only the objects you care about, reducing noise and fatigue while walking.

How light warning works
The page samples the live frame brightness and warns when the average intensity falls below a low-light threshold.
